Volunteers

With an intention of combating this menace of child labor, the volunteer of bachhpan planned to visit hotels, shacks, food outlets, business establishment, industries and other firms in the city to identify and rescue child labor.
It’s a matter of pride that our NGO demonstrates commitment to our neighbor through an effective and program focused on reducing child labor.
Vast team of volunteers at bachhpan works with a motto - “making education a reality for every child”.
Volunteers of bachhpan are mainly engaged into 2 categories of work.

•  Teaching staff
•  Campaigning staff

All together the major responsibilities the volunteers of bachhpan handles.

• Teaching children at slum
• Raising funds for the children
• Sharing information and cultural values with different
    people
We are proud that volunteers of gcet, adit, bvm, and icct solidarity support this noble cause.
Volunteers here are invaluable supporters. We have a team of 150+ volunteers of kind, flexible, friendly profile. They handle all together 150+ children in all the four slums area running can help the NGO in anand, vidhyanagar.
These volunteers not just convince their parents in sending their children to school but also in unpacking their inner abilities and development of the children as a social being.

Volunteers of bachhpan works with an aim of - “Naanhe haath kalam ke saath”
