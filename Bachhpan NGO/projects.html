Projects

AADHAAR

Under project “AADHAAR” we aim at providing complete support to children who are child labourer. We enroll such students in best schools in cities and take care of their every needs : from tuition fees to books and food to clothes.
A special team of volunteer mentros such students and monitors their need and progress.In just a period of one year we have changed 22 lives forever by ensuring their every educational expenses as well as daily needs till they mature.

Bachhpan will always be there for them to provide equity and not charity.


PATHSHALA

With project Paathshala, we aim to take education to places where schools can't.
A dedicated team of volunteers provides education to 250+ children in different slums everyday through evening classes.

Also apart from academic activity,we take care of issues like Hygiene,morals etc.

"We ensure that every children get their fundamental right of getting Education..."


ABCD (Any Body Can Dream)

Who doesn’t want that ability to speak fluently in front of a crowd? Everyone of us does. “ABCD” is a new step by our team to provide not only academic upliftment to our students, but all-round personality development for these rising stars.

Under this project we provide our students with different types of books. Our volunteers have made a large collection of books for the students depending upon their age groups. This enables them to increase their vocabulary power along with their reading skills. This will surely help them to build their confidence and be very fruitful in their future.
